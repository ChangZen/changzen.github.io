Foundation in Business

This Foundation gives you the opportunity to study business in Malaysia, equipping you with the necessary foundation to pursue a future in business.
The programme emphasises on learning by doing, and therefore, students are regularly required to work on actual projects.

Diploma in Computer Science

This Computer Science diploma programme is designed to lay a strong foundation in computing skills on those wishing to become a Programmer and Software Engineer in the future.
Here, you will learn how to use some of the most popular programming languages to write codes for smart watches, robots, mobile app games, websites with e-commerce components, etc.

Degree in Business Administration

Studying a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) course provides skills that are useful for a wide variety of career paths. For example, industries related to business consultancy, franchising management, advertising and marketing, human resources and office administration.
As you can see, the versatility of BBA courses can enable you to apply for business and non-business related careers.
